# Sample Prompts

Use these prompts as acceptance checks while building the MVP.

## Schema Introspection

- List all tables available in the connected database.
- Show the columns for the `orders` table.
- Tell me which tables reference `customers`.

## Basic Retrieval

- Show all customers in California.
- List product names and prices for products in the Electronics category.
- Show the 10 most recent orders.

## Joins

- Show each order with the customer name and order date.
- List products ordered by customer Alice Johnson.
- Show all order items with product name, quantity, and unit price.

## Aggregations

- Show total revenue by customer.
- Show the top 5 products by units sold.
- Count orders by status.

## Time Filters

- Show orders placed in January 2026.
- Show revenue by day for the last 30 days.
- List customers who have not placed an order in the last 60 days.

## Clarification Cases

- Show the best customers.
- Show expensive products.
- Show recent orders.
